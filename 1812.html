1812: The Invasion of Canada

2017

1812: The Invasion of Canada is a historical strategy game based on the boardgame of the same name, and it is the sequel to 1775: Rebellion.

I was very heavily involved in this game. I personally worked on:

  • all 3D assets
  • particle effects and animations
  • map control shaders
  • camera scripts
  • dynamic tooltip scripts
  • game audio
