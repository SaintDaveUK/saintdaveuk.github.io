About Dave

Introduction

Hello, my name is Dave Horler.

I am a 3D Artist working in a dynamic and challenging role in video games. I am lucky enough to be working with studios all over the world. Currently, I am focused on developing my technical skills to create the best games that I can!


Experience

HexWar Games Ltd.

(2015 - present)

I started working at HexWar Games Ltd in Scotland in 2015. As the only 3D Artist, I work across our whole library of games on almost every aspect.

I am also responsible for working with external studios, and ensuring that outsourced content is consistent with the company's standards.


Paradox South

(2013 - 2014)

I completed a year-long 3D Artist internship with Paradox Interactive in Sweden. I created assets for the historical strategy games, Crusader Kings 2 and Europa Universalis 4.


University of Kent

(2011 - 2015)

I studied at the University of Kent until 2015, where I was awarded a first-class degree in Digital Arts BA (Hons).
