1775: Rebellion

2016

1775: Rebellion is a historical strategy game based on the boardgame of the same name.

My job was to design the characters, map and environment, and then implement them into Unity.

It was the first game I have worked on as the only 3D Artist, so it was a real learning experience, but I’m really proud of the impact that I have had on the game’s overall look.

The game also had to run on mobile (iOS and Android) and so I developed a range of scalable optimisation techniques to ensure that 1775: Rebellion looked its best from a high-end PC to a low-end tablet.
