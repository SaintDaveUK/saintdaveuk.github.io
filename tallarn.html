The Horus Heresy: Battle of Tallarn

2017

This was a PC and mobile game that HexWar Games Ltd developed. It was a licensed game using Games Workshop's famous Warhammer 40k setting.

My primary role in this game was supervising our outsourcing partners to ensure the hundreds of art assets were produced to a consistent artistic and technical standard.

Games Workshop is cautious with other companies producing their IP, so I am glad that the assets that I managed were all approved with very few issues.
