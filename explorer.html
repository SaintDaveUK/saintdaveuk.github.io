Explorer

2015

This was a project that I made in my spare time to get a better understanding of Quixel Suite and material IDs.

I used Julius's low-poly female as a base mesh to create a Zbrush sculpt.

I ended up making the highest-detailed low-poly model I have ever created. This allows it to fit seamlessly into very detailed game environments.

In her original design, I was worried that she looked too much like reboot Lara Croft. Along the line, she lost the tank top and the dark brown hair so she looked bit a little more original.

The whole process took me a little over one working week.
