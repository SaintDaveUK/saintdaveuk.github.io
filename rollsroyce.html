Rolls-Royce

2017

I wanted to make an interesting vehicle to practice my hard surface modelling skills. I decided upon an Armoured Rolls Royce, which was built in 1920 and used by the Irish National Army until 1944.

I rigged and animated the vehicle. I tried to give its movement a bit of personality (or as much personality as a lump of steel could have!).

I imported the car into Hearts of Iron 4 to show how it can be used as a game asset. The animations and particle effects are all linked up for the complete effect.
