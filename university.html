I spent four years studying Digital Arts BA at the University of Kent.

The course was mostly a focus on 3D graphics and programming, with elements from related disciplines such as film and design.



This is a Unity3D platformer I made with Aishling Mulhern for the Videogame Design module, inspired by the PS1 and N64 platformers that we played growing up. You are Chop, an ambitious pig desperate to escape the farm to save his bacon!

I created most of the art and animations, as well as game design and level scripting.
